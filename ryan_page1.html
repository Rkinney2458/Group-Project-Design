Didnt see the corner coming, need to not get hit by that bomb, trying breaking! Breaking allows you to slow your Kart to an eventuall stop by holding the B button. Breaking can allow you to make a turn you would have missed otherwise, and can be used to set up a drift for a vital burst of speed! Breaking is an integeral part of mastering the game and you cannot achieve top level play without use of this vital mechanic.
