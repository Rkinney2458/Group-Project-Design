About Acceleration

Acceleration and mastery of speed or your Kart in general in a critical mechanic of the game.
Our different charcters and Karts have different acceleration values, which can contribute to
your victory or defeat at that critical last minute.


Different Karts also have various different max speed values, whith Karts with a lower acceleration
typing having a higher maximum speed, acceleration is not affected by speed items within the game.
